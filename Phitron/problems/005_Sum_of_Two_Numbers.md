# Sum of Two Numbers

**Time Limit:** 1 second  
**Memory Limit:** 2.93 MB  

### Problem Statement
Take two integers `A` and `B` as input and output their summation.

### Constraints
`-10^9 ≤ A, B ≤ 10^9`

### Input Format
- You will be given two integers `A` and `B` separated by a space.

### Output Format
- Output their summation.

### Sample Input 1
`2 3`

### Sample Output 1
`5`

### Sample Input 2
`-10 5`

### Sample Output 2
`-5`

### Explanation
The program adds the two input integers and prints their sum as output.
